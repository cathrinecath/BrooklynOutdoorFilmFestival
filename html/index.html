Welcome to Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival

An amazing event in New York city showing incredible films

News and Announcement

This year we have twelve different films and will be shown from 5th through 8th August, from 6pm to midnight.
Each day 3 movies will be played. All of them are watchable from kids to adult. Be sure to bring your family and friends!
Tickets are free and open to everyone.
Please pre-register so that you can get your free tickets.

Film List

5th August

Justin and the Knights of Valour

Justin lives in a kingdom where bureaucrats rule and knights have been ousted. His dream is to be become one of the Knights of Valour, like his grandfather was, but his father Reginald, the chief counsel to the Queen, wants his son to follow in his footsteps and become a lawyer. After an inspiring visit to his beloved Grandmother and bidding farewell to his supposed lady-love Lara, Justin leaves home and embarks on a quest to become a knight...more

The Emoji

Inside each smartphone are these small faces and items called Emojis. Every Emoji means something different, but unfortunately, Gene, a so-called "Meh" Emoji, has a problem. He has all the expressions of the faces. Thinking that he is special in his own way, Gene partners up with smart Emo Emoji Jailbreak... more

The Son of Bigfoot

Teenage outsider Adam sets out on an epic and daring quest to uncover the mystery behind his long-lost dad, only to find out that he is none other than the legendary Bigfoot! He has been hiding deep in the forest for years to protect himself and his family from HairCo., a giant corporation eager to run scientific experiments with his special DNA... more

8th August
